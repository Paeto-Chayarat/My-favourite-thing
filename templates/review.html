My own review for each sunscreen!

Shiseido Senka perfect UV gel waterproof

This suncreen is the hero of my skin. It saved me from the arduous outdoor cheerleader training. It is waterproof and is not oily at all. It has a milky texture and dries quickly when applied.

Anessa, Perfect UV Sunscreen skincare milk

Anessa, just a replacement of Senka. They are the same in quality and feels the same. This come in handy when Senka is out of stock(often). However, this sunscreen emits a signature smell which I don't like.

MizuMi UV Water Serum

Mizumi, for me gives an extra light feeling. It feels like nothing when applied on face. And it is the only lightweight sunscreen that is not oily when it's on my face.

Banana Boat Sport Sunscreen Lotion

This is my main body sunscreen when doing outdoor activites(especially cheerleadung). This one is very sticky so I use it only when going outdoor for a long time. Despite the stickiness. this sunscreen helps a lot, protecting my arms and legs from burning.
